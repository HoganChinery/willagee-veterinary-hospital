Our services

Comprehensive veterinary care for dogs and cats — everyday wellness through to specialist surgery, all under one roof in Willagee.

Emergency Care

Pet emergency during opening hours? Call us straight away — we'll get you in as quickly as possible. Come to us first, don't wait.

Consultations

Thorough health checks, diagnosis and friendly, expert advice for pets of all ages.

Surgery

Routine and advanced soft-tissue procedures performed in our sterile operating theatre.

Vaccinations

Tailored vaccination programs to protect your pet against preventable disease.

Radiology

Digital X-ray imaging for fast, accurate diagnosis of injuries and conditions.

Ultrasound

Non-invasive ultrasound imaging to assess internal organs and soft tissue.

Dentistry

Scale, polish, extractions and dental care for fresher breath and healthier teeth.

Pathology

In-house laboratory testing for fast, reliable blood, urine and tissue results.

Nutrition

Tailored diet and weight-management plans for every age, breed and condition.

Desexing

Safe, routine desexing for dogs and cats, with attentive after-care.

Cat Boarding

A safe, comfortable and calm home-away-from-home for your cat.

House Calls

Veterinary visits in the comfort of your own home for pets who travel poorly.

Home Euthanasia

A gentle, peaceful and dignified goodbye in familiar surroundings at home.

Behaviour

Advice and support for behavioural concerns, for calmer, happier pets.

Orthopedic Services

Complex bone and joint procedures performed in-house by our experienced team.

General Health Conditions

Diagnosis and ongoing management of all general health conditions in dogs and cats.
