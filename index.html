Welcome to the family

Your neighbourhood vet, here for every wag and purr.

Willagee Veterinary Hospital is a full-service clinic caring for the pets of Willagee and the surrounding suburbs. We combine modern facilities — radiology, ultrasound, in-house pathology and a dedicated surgery — with genuine, unhurried care that puts both you and your pet at ease.

Whether it's a first puppy vaccination, a routine dental, or a complex orthopaedic procedure, we're here to help your animals live long, happy, healthy lives.
