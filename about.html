About our hospital

A neighbourhood veterinary hospital where modern medicine meets genuine, unhurried care for the pets of Willagee.

Our story

Here for every wag and purr.

Willagee Veterinary Hospital is a full-service clinic caring for the pets of Willagee and the surrounding suburbs. We believe great veterinary care is as much about reassurance and trust as it is about expertise.

Our friendly team takes the time to make every visit calm and stress-free — for your pet and for you. From a first puppy vaccination to gentle senior care, we're here for every step of the journey.

Our facilities

Modern care, all on site

Everything your pet needs for diagnosis and treatment, in one place.
